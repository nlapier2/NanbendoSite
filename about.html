About Us!

Welcome to the website of Nanbendo, a game and application development group founded by Benjamin Ward and Nathan LaPierre! We began working on projects together in 2013, but nothing was produced at first. Eventually, in 2014, we doubled down our focus and developed our first mobile game, Jumpy Deer. Jumpy Deer was initially released in February 2014 to the Android Marketplace, and passed the 100 download milestone in March. Also in March, we submitted Jumpy Deer to the Apple App Store. After Jumpy Deer, we plan to diversify our projects and work on some web applications and utilities in addition to our mobile games. All of our projects are free (although we may make better "pro" versions available for a price) and can be found on this website. Use the links at the top of the page to find our projects, please don't hesitate to support us if you like our work, and most importantly, enjoy!
